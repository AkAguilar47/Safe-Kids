Talking About Feelings:

  • It's important to tell someone you trust how you're feeling. Keeping feelings inside can make you feel worse.

Having a Routine:

  • Doing things at the same time every day, like eating breakfast and going to bed, can help you feel more normal and safe.

Exercise:

  • Moving your body, like playing outside or dancing, can make you feel happier.

Getting Enough Sleep:

  • Sleeping well at night helps you feel better during the day.

Relaxing:

  • Taking deep breaths or thinking about nice things can help calm you down if you're feeling stressed.

Asking for Help:

  • If you need extra help, talking to a teacher or counselor can be helpful.

Remember, it's okay to talk about your feelings and ask for help.
