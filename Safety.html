Feeling Uncomfortable:

  • If someone makes you feel scared, nervous, or uncomfortable, trust your feelings.

Secretive Behavior:

  • If an adult asks you to keep a secret from your parents or guardians, that's a red flag.

Inappropriate Touching:

  • If someone tries to touch you in a way that feels wrong or makes you uncomfortable, tell a trusted adult right away.

Offering Gifts:

  • Be cautious if a stranger offers you gifts, candy, or money, especially if they ask you to go somewhere with them.

Asking for Help:

  • If an adult you don't know asks you for help, like finding a lost pet or giving directions, find a trusted adult to help them instead.

Following You:

  • If someone you don't know is following you, go to a safe place and tell a trusted adult.

Online Interactions:

  • Be careful when talking to people online. Never share personal information like your address, phone number, or school.
