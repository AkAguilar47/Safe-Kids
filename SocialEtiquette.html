Social Etiquette

say Please and Thank You:

  • Use these polite words whenever asking for something or receiving something.

Take Turns:

  • Wait for your turn patiently in lines and when playing games.

Use Indoor Voice:

  • Keep your voice at a normal level; don't shout.

Respect Personal Space:

  • Give people enough space and don't push or shove.

Be Respectful:

  • Use kind words and be nice to others.

Listen and Follow Rules:

  • Pay attention to adults and follow their instructions.

Cover Your Mouth:

  • When you cough or sneeze, cover your mouth with a tissue or your elbow.

Stay with Your Group:

  • Always stay close to your parents or guardians.

Clean Up After Yourself:

  • Pick up your trash and clean up your area.

Ask for Help:

  • If you get lost or need help, find a trusted adult.

Hold the Door:

  • Hold the door open for others, especially if they are carrying something.

Offer Your Seat:

  • If you're sitting and see an elderly person or a pregnant woman standing, offer them your seat.
